What is Cuny Coin

Cuny Coin is an innovative payment network and a new kind of money for students.

CUNY coin is one of the most important inventions in all of Student history. For the first time ever, Cuny Students can send or receive or buy any Crypto in school via Cafteria Book Store and even vending machines, conveniently and without restriction. It’s the dawn of a better, more free campus.
